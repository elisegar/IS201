Elise's Musical Journey

My Love for Music

Music has always been a huge part of my life. My earliest memories are of listening to music in the car with my parents. One of my favorite CDs was "Bibbidi Bobbidi Bach"—a collection of Disney songs in the style of classical music. I've always loved listening to music, and would spend time laying down to listen as a child. My mom enrolled me in piano lessons when she discovered how much I loved music.

The Beginning Stages

I started piano lessons at seven years old. I loved practing and would spend hours at the piano. In middle school, I learned the viola and joined the school orchestra. In high school, I picked up the cello and continued in orchestra with that instrument instead. You can stil find me playing music today, whether it be through accompanying gigs or church callings.
